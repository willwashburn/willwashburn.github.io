  • Where I started

    In a previous life and in the days before swiping right, I co-founded Social Blendr with @khaliqgant. Our mission was to end loneliness. We failed; people are lonely. It was fun, though.

  • Before that I flew to the opposite side of the world to sell something I don't drink in a language I never spoke; suck it haters! Bravado was not something we lacked. Funding, licences and a general plan, however, were.

  • However, my first startup will always have a special place in my heart. We purchased text books on Amazon and sold them on campus at a significant discount from the local bookstore. At the time, the alpha was pretty huge!
